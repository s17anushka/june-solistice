# 🌅 The Longest Test

> *A narrative game for the June Solstice Game Jam.*

You are a civil servant. It is the longest day of the year. A 1950s computer terminal has awoken in the basement — and inside it, the ghost of **Alan Turing** has questions.

Answer them before the sun sets.

---

## How to Play

Open `solstice-turing-game.html` in any browser. No install, no dependencies, no algorithms required (probably).

Choose your answers carefully. Wrong ones cost you daylight. ☀️ → 🌑

---

## Themes

- ☀️ **June Solstice** — a live solar arc tracks your remaining daylight
- 🤖 **Alan Turing** — every question is an ode to the father of computer science
- 🌈 **Pride Month** — because one colour was never going to be enough
- ✊ **Juneteenth** — on liberation, memory, and what freedom actually means
- 🍣 **International Sushi Day** — sniff the fish. You know what to do.
- 🩴 **National Flip-Flop Day** — the pineapples are load-bearing.

---

## Built With

Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks. No build step. Just vibes and a typewriter effect.
